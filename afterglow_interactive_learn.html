Physical meaning — short

The condensate Σ is a memory variable. At the crossing r = 1, Ψ vanishes by strict detailed balance of the CDM↔X exchange (antisymmetry Ψ(1/r) = −Ψ(r) forces it). Early universe r ≫ 1: Ψ > 0, Q < 0, CDM loads the hidden fluid. Future r ≪ 1: Ψ < 0, Q > 0, hidden fluid unloads back into CDM. Extrema of Ψ are at r = 2 ± √3 with |Ψ|max = 2√3/9 ≈ 0.3849.
