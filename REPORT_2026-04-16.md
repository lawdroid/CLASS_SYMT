## What Step 4 actually required

The PHASE1B2_PATCHES.md described a two-line fix (`dy[index_bi_rho_cdm] += cdm_source`), but stock CLASS computes CDM analytically as ρ_cdm = Ω_cdm · a^(−3) — there was no `index_bi_rho_cdm` in the integration vector. Promoting CDM to a differential equation required five edits:

1. **`include/background.h`** — added `index_bi_rho_cdm_aft` to the integration struct
2. **`source/background.c` (index allocation)** — `class_define_index()` gated on `has_afterglow && has_cdm`
3. **`source/background.c` (initial conditions)** — ρ_cdm(a_ini) = Ω_cdm · H₀² · a_ini^(−3)
4. **`source/background.c` (`background_derivs`)** — base ODE `dρ_cdm/dloga = −3ρ_cdm` plus `+= cdm_source` from Eq. 44
5. **`source/background.c` (`background_functions`)** — reads CDM from ODE when β≠0, analytic formula otherwise

The β≠0 gate in edit 5 was caught during testing: without it, the ODE integrator introduces ~2×10⁻⁴ numerical drift vs the exact analytic formula, even when the back-reaction term is zero. With the gate, β=0 is bit-identical to pre-patch output (within CLASS's own threading non-determinism of ~2.4×10⁻⁴).

**Verification that back-reaction is active:** β=0.1 produces a 300% shift in Cl relative to β=0 — the coupling is physically operative.
